WHAT IS MATEX?

FOR exchange students and local students WHO are seeking international connections, MATEX is the go-to matching app, THAT is designed to ease the transition into a new country. Moreover, MATEX enables users to find their ideal study mate based on shared interests, hobbies, and personal preferences. UNLIKE conventional social platforms, OUR product focuses specifically on facilitating meaningful connections between individuals from different cultural backgrounds. Whether you're an exchange student looking to immerse yourself in the local culture or a local student eager to expand your global network, MATEX is the perfect platform for forging cross-cultural friendships.

MATEX - KEY FEATURES

  • Personalized Matching: Matching users based on interests, hobbies, and preferences, ensuring meaningful connections
  • Cross-Cultural Connections: Connects exchange and local students fostering cultural exchange and understanding
  • Language Practice: Opportunities for language practice with users from different linguistic backgrounds
